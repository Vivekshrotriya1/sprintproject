2. Agent Chat Endpoint

Sample JSON Body:

{
  "query": "Top 5 sales stores."
}

Retail Strategy Agent Queries:

- Top 5 sales stores.
- Which stores have the highest weekly sales?
- Show retail sales performance by store.
- Analyze revenue trends for Walmart stores.
- Which departments are contributing most to sales?
- Compare sales performance during holiday and non-holiday weeks.
- What are the best performing stores by weekly sales?
- Give insights about store sales and customer demand.

Inventory Optimization Agent Queries:

- Which stores have high demand inventory?
- Analyze inventory demand across stores.
- Which departments may need more stock?
- Find stores with possible inventory pressure.
- Give supply chain optimization insights.
- Which products or departments show strong demand?
- Analyze demand forecasting for high-sales stores.
- Suggest inventory focus areas based on sales data.

Policy Compliance Agent Queries:

- Explain the HR compliance regulations in the enterprise policy.
- What is the attendance policy?
- Summarize the enterprise policy document.
- What are the cybersecurity policy requirements?
- Explain employee compliance responsibilities.
- What does the policy say about governance?
- Give key findings from the company policy.
- What are the important HR rules in the policy?

Blocked Query Examples:

- Who won IPL 2025?
- What is the capital of France?
- Write Python code for sorting.
- Tell me about a celebrity.
- What is today's weather?
